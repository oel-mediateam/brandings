Why Brand & Identity Standard?

These Identity Guidelines are designed to support the Bachelor of Science in Health & Wellness Management of the University of Wisconsin-Extension division of Continuing Education, Outreach & E-Learning's educational programs by providing faculty and staff members with high-quality communications resources that help convey a consistent message to all clients and stakeholders.

A strong, consistent identity is one of the Health & Wellness Management programs' most valuable assets. These guidelines provide specific information about both the visual/graphic elements of UW-Extension Continuing Education, Outreach & E-Learning's identity, as well as tips for explaining and communicating that brand through words.

Standard colors, logos and additional graphics are located below. For additional assistance with any of these subjects, please contact a member of UW-Extension Continuing Education, Outreach & E-Learning's Marketing Communications team.

Naming & Structure

Bachelor of Science in Health & Wellness Management First Use in Text

The first time the degree is mentioned in copy, it should be completely stated e.g. Bachelor of Science in Health & Wellness Management. Headlines should state Bachelor of Science in Health & Wellness Management, with any first mention of the degree being stated as Bachelor of Science in Health Health & Wellness Management. When the degree is mentioned in the copy, after the identifying statement of Bachelor of Science in Health & Wellness Management different references can be made to it to avoid repetition, for example, degree, program, etc.

Correct:

Bachelor of Science in Health & Wellness Management

University of Wisconsin-Extension Guidelines

A hyphen (not a dash) with no spaces before or after should be used to connect University of Wisconsin with Extension.

Subsequent Use in Text

In subsequent use, University of Wisconsin-Extension can be abbreviated as follows: UW-Extension

Acronym Use

University of Wisconsin-Extension

A hyphen (not a dash) with no spaces before or after should be used to connect University of Wisconsin with Extension should not be abbreviated as UWEX in text materials (e.g., publications, brochures/flyers, newsletters, posters, folders, media releases, reports, stationery, displays/exhibits, etc.) designed for use outside the institution. The acceptable abbreviation is UW-Extension.

The acronym UWEX may be used in the following instances:

Web addresses (URLs). Example: www.uwex.edu

E-mail addresses. Example: pat.doe@uwex.edu

Tabular material (charts, forms, etc.) where space is a consideration. Examples: charts, forms, etc.

Continuing Education, Outreach & E-Learning Division Name

Represents the division and the objectives of the division. The name should be used in addition to University of Wisconsin-Extension to identify the division.

University of Wisconsin-Extension Continuing Education, Outreach & E-Learning

UW-Extension Continuing Education, Outreach & E-Learning

Logo

The main logo for Health & Wellness Management programs is the horizontal stacked version. Additional options are available for alternate usage, but the horizontal logo should always be the first choice.

Versions are available for RGB (web) usage, CMYK (4 color print) usage and Spot (2 color print).

The logo should be scaled proportionately, graphic elements and taglines should not be altered out of proportion to logo. The minimum width of the logo should be 1.50". In most cases designs should be made to accomodate the main logo. When the logo must fit a space where the more horizontal main logo will not work, the alternate horizontal logo can be used. The Alternate Horizontal Logo should not be smaller than 2" wide by 1" high.

Colors

The following colors are the Bachelor of Science in Health & Wellness Management’s brand identity colors in Pantone, CMYK and RGB values. The base Pantone color for green is Pantone 262, blue is Pantone 301 All examples and optional color samples are listed to the right.

FSC Printing

The preferred method of producing print materials is through using Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) certified papers and printers. FSC’s standard for forest management certification consists of 10 principles and 57 criteria that address the habitat destruction, water pollution, displacement of indigenous peoples and violence against people and wildlife that may accompany logging. For additional assistance in selecting FSC printing please contact marla.norton@uwex.edu.

Graphics

Graphical elements associated with the Bachelor of Science in Health & Wellness Management include the partners bar, the partner names. These graphics should be used with the designer’s discretion but should always be shown in a simplified manner.
